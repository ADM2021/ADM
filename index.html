Technical Case Study

Project: Celestial Extraction Corp (CEC) – 2026 Orbital Audit

The Challenge: As the space-mining industry matures, "Celestial Extraction Corp" required a high-level financial oversight tool to evaluate mission efficiency across multiple orbital sectors. The goal was to transform raw mission telemetry into a strategic command center for C-Suite executives to identify **"Alpha"** in a high-risk, multi-billion dollar frontier economy.

Phase 1: Engineering the Synthetic Ledger (Python)

Because real-world orbital financial data is highly proprietary, I engineered a custom dataset using Python (Pandas & NumPy). I designed a schema representing 10,000 "Extraction Events," incorporating variables like resource rarity (Helium-3, Platinum), market spot prices, and sector-specific operational costs. I injected realistic dependencies—ensuring that Helium-3 missions yielded higher margins but carried greater logistical overhead.

Phase 2: Visual Architecture & UI/UX (Tableau)

The dashboard was designed as a "Solar-Tech" Command Center, moving away from traditional corporate aesthetics to an immersive, holographic HUD experience. Key features include the Resource Treemap for instant regional revenue visibility and the Executive Quadrant (Scatter Plot) developed with an ROI Factor metric to distinguish "Stars" from "Problem Sectors."

Phase 3: C-Suite Strategic Utility

This tool serves three critical functions: Capital Allocation (justifying investments in high-cost but efficient sectors like Near-Earth Orbit), Risk Mitigation (flagging underperforming missions), and Market Intelligence (timing resource sales based on volatility trends).
